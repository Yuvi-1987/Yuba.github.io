Services (Controller/CFO Advisory)

Choose a monthly engagement or a focused sprint. Everything is scoped, documented, and designed to be maintainable.

30‑Day Finance Stabilization

Ideal when close is inconsistent, cash visibility is weak, or reporting isn’t trusted.

  • Close calendar + checklists
  • Key reconciliations with working papers
  • AR/WIP triage and billing rhythm
  • Owner reporting pack (P&L / BS / Cash / Projects)

Output: a stable process + documentation + next 60‑day roadmap.

Fractional Controller (Monthly)

Ongoing ownership of close and core finance operations.

  • Month-end close ownership
  • Accruals, cutoff, and policy discipline
  • AR collections cadence and controls
  • WIP/billing integrity (project firms)

Best for: firms that need a steady hand and stronger controls.

Part‑Time CFO (Monthly/Quarterly)

Forward-looking finance and decision support for owners.

  • Budget + rolling forecast
  • 13‑week cash runway
  • Pricing/margin/utilization insights
  • Owner/board-ready reporting

Best for: growth periods, hiring decisions, and margin pressure.

Automation Sprint (4–6 weeks)

Build a source-of-truth dataset + dashboards + refresh workflow with controls.

Data
  • Data extraction (accounting + timesheets + expenses)
  • Validation checks
  • Documentation of sources
Reporting
  • Power BI dashboards
  • Owner pack metrics
  • Project margin views
Controls
  • Approvals preserved
  • Audit trail retained
  • Refresh steps documented

Note: Automation is only implemented where it reduces manual effort without increasing reporting risk.

What I don’t provide

I do not offer audit, review, or compilation services in New Brunswick unless properly licensed/authorized. My work focuses on internal controllership, reporting, cash flow, project margin visibility, and finance process/automation.

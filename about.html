How I work

A simple, durable approach — designed to survive busy seasons, staff changes, and growth.

1) Diagnose

Understand the business model, revenue cycle, project/WIP flow, and the current close. Identify what’s breaking cash and trust.

2) Stabilize

Build a clean month-end routine: reconciliations, schedules, templates, and clear ownership. Fix the basics that drive accuracy.

3) Automate

Automate repeatable reporting with controls (inputs, checks, and audit trail). Reduce manual exports and spreadsheet fragility.

4) Improve (continuous)

Once the foundation is solid, we tighten forecasting, improve AR performance, and add deeper project/client analytics. The goal is a finance function that helps the business make faster, better decisions.
